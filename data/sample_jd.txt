Job Title: Senior Backend Engineer (Python)

About the role:
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to design and build scalable APIs and data
pipelines for our recruitment-tech platform. You will work closely with the ML team to
ship production services that power candidate matching at scale.

Must-have requirements:
- 4+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python and at least one web framework (FastAPI, Django, or Flask)
- Hands-on experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL/MySQL) and SQL optimization
- Experience designing and consuming REST APIs
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS or GCP)
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field

Nice-to-have:
- Experience with vector databases or semantic search (FAISS, Pinecone, Weaviate)
- Familiarity with Docker and Kubernetes
- Experience with asynchronous task queues (Celery, RQ)
- Prior experience in HR-tech, fintech, or other data-heavy domains
- Contributions to open-source projects

Seniority: Senior (4-8 years)
Location: Remote (India)
Notice period preference: Immediate to 30 days
